Housing by Demographic

Residence type is strongly correlated with key demographic factors such as membership, sex, and age. The charts below illustrate how these groups are distributed across different housing situations.

Residence by Membership

The data shows a clear pattern: "Freshers" overwhelmingly reside in Hostels, while "Associates" and "Elders" are highly concentrated in Rented housing.

Residence by Sex

While Rented housing is the most common for both genders, there is a notable split in other types. Females are more likely to live in Hostels, while males are more likely to live in Other/Minority housing.

Residence by Age Group

Age is a powerful predictor of residence. The 18-24 age group is heavily represented in Hostels, while Rented housing is the dominant choice for almost every other age group.
