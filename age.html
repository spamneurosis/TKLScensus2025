Age Distribution by Demographic Group

Age demographics vary significantly across different membership tiers and between genders. The charts below provide a more detailed look at these distributions.

Age Distribution by Membership

This chart shows that "Freshers" and "Old Timers" are concentrated in the younger age groups, while "Elders" are predominantly in the older age brackets. "Infants" are entirely within the 0-5 age group.

Age Pyramid by Sex

The age pyramid reveals a strong concentration of both males and females in the 18-24 age group. Females have a larger population in this group, while males are more prominent in the 30-39 age group.
