Primary Reasons for Arrival

The data shows a clear pattern in why people have come to Shillong. Education is the overwhelmingly dominant reason, followed by employment and family-related motives. Other reasons, such as health and religion, make up a very small percentage.

Purpose by Demographic

The reason for coming to Shillong is strongly correlated with key demographic factors such as membership, sex, and age. The charts below illustrate these relationships.

Purpose by Membership

Membership tiers show distinct reasons for arrival. The "Freshers" group is almost exclusively in Shillong for education, while "Elders" and "Old Timers" show a greater diversity of reasons, including employment and family.

Purpose by Sex

While education is the top reason for both males and females, the data shows differences in other categories. Males are more likely to have arrived for employment, while females are more likely to have come for family/relatives.

Purpose by Age Group

The age of arrival strongly dictates the purpose. The youngest age groups are almost entirely here for education. The "Employment" and "Family/Relatives" categories grow in prominence as the age group increases.

Year of Arrival Trends

The trend of people coming to Shillong, primarily for education, has seen a sharp increase in recent years, especially from 2022 onwards.
