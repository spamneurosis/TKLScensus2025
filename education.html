Education by Demographic Group

Educational attainment varies significantly across different membership tiers and between genders. The charts below illustrate these key differences.

Education by Membership

Educational attainment varies significantly across different membership tiers. The "Freshers" and "Infants" groups are dominated by pre-high school and high school qualifications, while "Old Timers" have a much higher proportion of graduate and post-graduate degrees.

Education by Sex

Gender plays a role in educational attainment. Females are more likely to have pursued a science or arts graduate degree, while males have a higher proportion of higher secondary and pre-high school qualifications.

Years of Schooling by Demographic

The overall years of schooling data provides a broader view. The median years of schooling is 15.0 years for both genders and "Freshers" have an average of 13.82 years, while "Old Timers" have 14.38 years.
