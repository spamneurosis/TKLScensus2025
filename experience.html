Rating by Demographic Group

While overall satisfaction is high, there are subtle differences in ratings across membership tiers, age groups, and gender. The charts below illustrate these variations.

Rating by Membership

Satisfaction is high across all membership tiers, with "Old Timers" and "Elders" giving the highest ratings. "Freshers" have a slightly wider distribution of ratings, indicating a more varied experience.

Rating by Sex

Males give a slightly higher proportion of 10/10 ratings compared to females. However, females are more likely to give a rating of 9/10, showing a very high level of overall satisfaction for both genders.

Rating by Age Group

The younger population, particularly those aged 18-24, gives the highest proportion of 10/10 ratings. The older age groups, while still highly positive, show a slightly more even distribution of ratings.
