Employment by Demographic Group

Employment status is strongly linked to membership and age. The charts below illustrate how these groups are distributed across different employment categories.

Employment by Membership

The data shows that "Freshers" are almost entirely students, while "Elders" and "Old Timers" show a greater diversity in employment status, with significant percentages in the employed and self-employed categories.

Employment by Sex

While both genders are predominantly students, there are differences in other categories. Males are more likely to be employed, while females have a higher proportion of self-employed individuals.

Employment by Age Group

Age is the strongest indicator of employment status. The "Student" category is dominant for all age groups under 25. The employed and self-employed categories grow in prominence with increasing age.
