Religious Affiliation by Demographics

This section explores the relationship between religious affiliation and other key demographic factors, including membership tier, gender, and age group.

Religious Makeup by Membership Tier

The stacked bar chart shows the religious breakdown within each membership tier. While Baptists are the majority in all groups, "Elders" and "Associates" have a higher proportion of Catholics compared to "Freshers."

Religious Affiliation by Gender

This chart illustrates the religious breakdown for male and female populations. While both genders are predominantly Baptist, females are slightly more likely to be Catholic or SDA, while males have a slightly larger representation in the Baptist and Other/Minority categories.

Religious Affiliation by Age Group

The age distribution of religious groups is varied. The Baptist population is spread across all age groups, with a large concentration in the 18-24 category. In contrast, Catholicism has a more even spread, and other religious groups have smaller, distinct age profiles.
