The Influx: A Look at Recent Arrivals

The most striking trend in the data is the massive increase in new arrivals to Shillong in the last few years. The number of people arriving annually has surged dramatically, with a significant spike in 2023, 2024, and 2025.

Demographics of New Arrivals

This section explores the demographic composition of new arrivals, showing how the profile of migrants has shifted over time, with a large number of "Freshers" arriving in the last year.

Membership Tiers of Arrivals

The stacked bar chart shows a clear shift. "Old Timers" were a dominant portion of arrivals until 2024. In 2025, a massive wave of "Freshers" and "Infants" arrived, fundamentally changing the demographic composition of new migrants.

Gender of Arrivals Over Time

While the gender balance of arrivals has generally been consistent, there is a clear recent increase in both male and female arrivals. Both genders show a sharp spike in arrivals in the latest years, reflecting the overall migration trend.

Cumulative Arrivals

The cumulative line chart provides a long-term view of the population growth. The curve is relatively flat until around 2020, at which point it begins to climb steeply, accelerating dramatically in 2023.
